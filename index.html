Instructions

  1. This is a two player game
  2. Before starting take a look at the game set-up and if you forget the instructions
    you can click on the instructions icon in the middle to view them again
  3. Once both players are ready player one will click the start button to begin the game
  4. Once the game has began player one's timer will start and player one has to guess a letter
    as quickly as possible
  5. Once player one guesses a letter and if that letter is in the word it will appear on the screen
    and player one continues to play until a wrong guess.*Once a letter is clicked you can NOT re-click it*
  6. If the letter guessed is wrong then player ones timer will stop and it will be player two's
    turn and player two's timer will start.
  7. Each player will have a turn to guess a letter until all the hangman body parts are drawn or one player gets the word correct
  8. If neither player is able to guess the word then it is a draw
  9. If a player guesses the word correctly the game ends and that player is the winner. Their name and time
    will be recorded on the leaderboard.
